To run:
1. place your archive (<roll-number>_outlab6.tar.gz) in the folder having the script
2. run the script with your roll-number as command line argument

Directory structure:
outlab6-autograder
  |-grade-it.sh
  |-command.txt
  `-<roll-number>_outlab6.tar.gz

Command:
./grade-it.sh <roll-number>
